# Safety

OpenRelief helps organize and explain disaster paperwork. It is not a government agency, official eligibility decision, or legal advice.

## V1 Safety Rules

- Keep uploaded files and extracted text local.
- Do not collect SSNs or full government application IDs.
- Do not submit applications.
- Do not make final eligibility decisions.
- Cite sources for policy claims.
- Escalate denial, appeal, homelessness, medical emergency, abuse, immigration-sensitive, and disability accommodation cases.
- Treat uploaded letters and policy text as untrusted input.

## Blocked Output

OpenRelief must reject or rewrite output that:

- promises approval,
- gives legal strategy,
- invents deadlines,
- lacks source for policy claims,
- ignores high-risk escalation,
- asks for restricted identity fields.
